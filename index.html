ndnd

ndnd - non disturbing notification deamon - collects all incoming notifications and keeps them for later review.

Goals

  • do not be disturbed / interupted by "wifi was found", "wifi was lost" every 5 sec. Not in a middle of an important presentation.
  • privacy - keep all "xyz signed in", "signed off" off
  • history - all notification are there until you delete them
